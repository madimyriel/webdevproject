Education

I am currently working on a Bachelors in Communications with an emphasis in Advertising from Brigham Young University. I am in the Creative Track and specialize in Art Direction.

Work Experience

I have mainly worked in the non-profit industry for the last 5 years. I have lived in Kenya, Fiji, Vanuatu, Tonga and Samoa with my company and continue my work with them as a project manager. My passion and love for humanitarian work has often taken precedence over traditions, which is why I won't graduate from my university studies until much later than the average student. Oops!

Aside from my non-profit work, I have worked as a freelance designer and photographer for the last decade. My passions surround film photography, oil painting, embroidery, and custom stationery. Click "Projects" above to view some of my latest work!
